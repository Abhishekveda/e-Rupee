What makes e₹ Bridge unique — the AI layer

Every other CBDC bridge PoC is just a payment pipe. e₹ Bridge embeds Claude AI directly into the compliance and risk workflow — the exact pain points that make banks reluctant to adopt new payment rails. This isn't AI for marketing. It solves three real regulatory problems.

01
FEMA Code Intelligence
Users don't know P0102 from P0103. Wrong codes trigger RBI compliance reviews. Claude maps plain English to the correct code automatically, reducing errors to near-zero.
02
Pre-Transfer Risk Engine
Claude analyses every transfer before it executes — amount vs purpose consistency, velocity checks, LRS proximity. Banks get AI-generated risk scores without building their own models.
03
Regulatory Q&A Assistant
Users ask questions in plain English. Claude answers with RBI-accurate regulatory guidance — reducing customer support load and improving compliance understanding across the diaspora.
