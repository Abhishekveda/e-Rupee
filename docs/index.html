Alignment with RBI policy objectives

Payments Vision 2025

Directly implements RBI's stated goal of CBDC for cross-border payment processing and settlement — June 2022 vision document.

e-Rupee wholesale pilot

Mirrors e₹-W interbank settlement architecture. Bridge relay is equivalent to the NDS-OM settlement mechanism.

Project Dunbar / mBridge

Same CBDC-to-CBDC bridge concept as BIS Project Dunbar — but open-source and developer-accessible for bank evaluation.

FEMA & LRS compliance

Mandatory FEMA purpose codes per RBI Master Direction. Architecture enforces $250,000 LRS annual cap per sender wallet.
